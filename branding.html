Logo Serg Steampunk

 

Sergey Drozdov is an artist who makes unique steampunk dolls. In his works he uses natural leather, polymer clay, natural hair, wood, metal.

The logo reflects the uniqueness of the dolls. The character is represented in the logo, gear wheel is located above his head, which symbolizes the work of the artist with metal. In the head of the character is the key that opens his mind to create works. Also in the logo we can see the steam from the pipes, glasses in the style of steampunk, and numerous threads that tie everything together.

Black and White
